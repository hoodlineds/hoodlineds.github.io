With hundreds of coffee shops to choose from, you don't have to walk very far to grab a cup of coffee in Boston. But when it comes to getting the coffee you love - like, Dunkin' Donuts, Starbucks and Au Bon Pain - how far do you actually have to go?

Using Yelp data to locate the geographical positioning of Boston's most abundant coffee shop chains, Hoodline crunched the numbers to determine just how close these outposts exist in relation to one another - and how Boston compares to other cities.

Not surprisingly, Boston's most prolific coffee shop chain is Dunkin' Donuts, which is the country's largest coffee chain, with 7941 stores nationwide. There are 100 Dunkin' Donuts locations in Boston, and according to the most recent census projections, that means there's roughly one Dunkin' Donuts per 6731 residents (not including daily commuters and tourists).
