With hundreds of coffee shops to choose from, you don't have to walk very far to grab a cup of coffee in Atlanta. But when it comes to getting the coffee you love - like, Starbucks, Dunkin' Donuts and Caribou Coffee - how far do you actually have to go?

Using Yelp data to locate the geographical positioning of Atlanta's most abundant coffee shop chains, Hoodline crunched the numbers to determine just how close these outposts exist in relation to one another - and how Atlanta compares to other cities.

Not surprisingly, Atlanta's most prolific coffee shop chain is Starbucks, which is the country's largest coffee chain, with 12078 stores nationwide. There are 64 Starbucks locations in Atlanta, and according to the most recent census projections, that means there's roughly one Starbucks per 7383 residents (not including daily commuters and tourists).
